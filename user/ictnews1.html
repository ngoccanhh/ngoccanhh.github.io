Huawei's surprise kick is a golden opportunity for Samsung to rise in the 5G network race

The difficulty of this person is an opportunity for others. Samsung is in a very important opportunity to gain market share in the global 5G network equipment market after Huawei is clamped down by the US governmen<

Against the backdrop of Huawei being isolated from the US and not cooperating with many US companies, analysts say this is a better opportunity for Huawei's competitors in telecommunications and smartphones to rise or At least create far-reaching distance for Huawei.

Samsung, despite being one of the largest smartphone manufacturers in the world, is struggling in the global network equipment market. In contrast, Chinese rival Huawei is leading the way.
